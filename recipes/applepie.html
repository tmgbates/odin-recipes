Apple Pie

Description

An apple pie is a fruit pie in which the principal filling ingredient is apples. It is often served with whipped cream, ice cream, custard or cheddar cheese

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Pie
  • Custard
  • Crust
  • Whipped cream
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Chill the ingredients
  2. Combine the shortening and flour
  3. Creating a ball of dough
  4. Divide the dough
  5. Peel and core the apples
