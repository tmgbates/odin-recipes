Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of lasagne baked with meat or vegetables and a cheese sauce. It is often pasta in the form of sheets or wide strips.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheese sauce
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
