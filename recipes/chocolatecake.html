Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a cake flavoured with melted chocolate and cocoa powder; it is topped with chocolate icing and various other sweets.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Cake
  • Baking powder
  • Fudge
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix butter, baking powder, and sugar together
  2. Whisk until made into a batter
  3. Put into a cake tin
  4. Bake at 350F until cooked
  5. Wait until cool then top with icing
